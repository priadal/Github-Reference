How to Create a Live Website Using Github

Step 1:

While on the Github homepage, click on your profile icon in the top right corner. This will lead to a dropdown menu, click on "Your repositories".

Step 2:

Now you should be on your "Your repositories" page. Here you will click on the "New" green button that features a little book icon.

Step 3:

This should bring you to to a page titled "Create a new repository".
